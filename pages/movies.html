Welcome to My Favorite Movies

Unfortunately, movie is not my thing because I am unluckily not a movie person. I do not enjoy watching movies and I do not have any favorite movies. I prefer to spend my free time engaging in activities that are related to football, which is my passion. However, I understand that movies can be a great source of entertainment for many people and I respect their preferences. If you are a movie person, I encourage you to explore different genres and find the movies that resonate with you the most!

That been said, recently I have been watching a movie called "The Goalkeeper's Journey" which is a documentary about the life of a goalkeeper and the challenges they face. It provides insights into the world of football from the perspective of a goalkeeper and it has been a great way for me to learn more about the position I play and the experiences of other goalkeepers. I highly recommend it to anyone interested in football or looking for an inspiring story about perseverance and dedication!

Thanks to Netflix subscription, I am able to watch some movie that caught my attention. Below is the list of my top 3 movies:

  • The Goalkeeper's Journey
  • S.W.A.T Series
  • Lucifer
